not really deserving of a script file, but hey, calculate share of votes
that remain needed win at final tally.

let $y = a b$ be percentages of current votes $x = p$ of two leading
parties, so $a+b \le 1$ and $p \le 1$. want to know what portion of $q =
1 - p$ is needed for $a \ge b$, by normalizing ~(%+/) y~ to ignore
third party & write-in votes.

#+begin_src J :session :exports code
V =: -.@[ %~ 0.5 - (*(%+/))
sts =: ;:'PA    NV    GA    NC    AZ'
eot =:    0.87  0.75  0.96  0.95  0.86
cvd =:    0.484 0.493 0.492 0.487 0.505
cvr =:    0.505 0.487 0.496 0.501 0.481
ord =: /: cal =: eot V"0 1 cvd ,. cvr
prj =: }."1 ,/"1 ] 6j3 ": ord { cal
#+end_src

#+RESULTS:
: 0.425 0.575
: 0.491 0.509
: 0.549 0.451
: 0.571 0.429
: 0.635 0.365

So,

#+begin_src J :session :exports both :verb 0!:1
('ST';'votes';'joe   don') ,: (ord{>sts) ; (,.ord{eot) ; prj
#+end_src
